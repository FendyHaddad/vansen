1. Who we are

Vansen is operated by Vankode Technology, a company registered in Malaysia. For the purposes of applicable data-protection law, Vankode Technology is the controller of the personal data described in this policy. You can reach us about privacy at support@vankode.com.

2. What we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — your email address and name, and, if you sign in with Google, the basic profile information Google provides.
  • Date of birth — collected once during onboarding to verify that you meet our 18+ eligibility requirement, and retained as evidence of that check.
  • Content — the prompts and images you submit (Inputs), the images you generate or edit (Outputs), and saved edits.
  • Transaction data — your Stripe customer identifier, subscription and purchase history, and your credit ledger. We do not receive or store your full card number — Stripe handles card details.
  • Moderation records — where content is flagged, we keep evidence of the flagged material and any strikes applied, for enforcement and appeals.
  • Technical data — information generated in the ordinary operation of the Service through our infrastructure providers, such as IP address, device and browser information, and timestamps, used for security and reliability.

3. Why we use it and our legal bases

Where the EU or UK General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to create and operate your account, provide generation and editing features, and process billing.
  • Legal obligation — to verify age eligibility, keep financial and tax records, and respond to lawful requests.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure the Service, prevent fraud and abuse, enforce our policies, and improve the Service, balanced against your rights.
  • Consent — only where we specifically ask for it, such as any future optional cookies or optional use of your content.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use it for third-party advertising, and we do not train AI models on your content without your opt-in consent.

4. Automated moderation

To keep the Service safe and lawful, content is screened by automated moderation before a generation is charged or sent to a provider. Repeated violations can lead to an automated account suspension. Where an automated decision significantly affects you, you can request human review and contest the outcome by contacting support@vankode.com.

5. Who we share it with

We share personal data with service providers who process it on our behalf and only on our instructions:

  • Supabase — hosting, authentication, and storage (infrastructure hosted on Amazon Web Services in the ap-southeast-1 region, Singapore).
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • OpenAI — image generation and automated moderation scanning of prompts and images.
  • Google — image generation.
  • fal.ai — image generation and editing.

When you run a generation or edit, the prompt and any image you provide are shared with the model provider you selected for that request, solely to perform it. We may also disclose personal data to authorities where legally required, and to a successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. International transfers

Our data is hosted in Singapore, and our providers may process data in other locations, including the United States and the European Union. Where personal data is transferred across borders in a way that triggers legal safeguards, we rely on appropriate mechanisms such as the European Commission's standard contractual clauses or an equivalent safeguard.

7. How long we keep it

  • Account data — for the life of your account.
  • Content — until you delete it, or until your account is deleted.
  • Media on a lapsed subscription — purged after a 30-day grace period following lapse.
  • Moderation evidence — retained for up to 12 months after an enforcement action, for appeals and legal defence, or longer where the law requires.
  • Billing and tax records — retained as required by law, typically up to seven (7) years.
  • Signed media links — expire 7 days after they are issued.

8. Your rights

European Union and United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data; to restrict or object to processing; to data portability; to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent; and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

California (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, to correct it, and to opt out of its sale or sharing — noting that we do not sell or share personal information — and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

Malaysia (PDPA 2010)

You have the right to access and correct your personal data, to withdraw consent, and to limit the processing of your personal data, in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010.

Everyone else

Wherever you live, we will honour access, correction, and deletion requests on the same basis.

To exercise your rights, email support@vankode.com, or delete your account directly using the in-app account controls. We may need to verify your identity before acting, and we will respond within 30 days or any shorter period required by law.

9. Cookies and local storage

We use only what is strictly necessary to run the Service: an authentication session stored in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in, and cookies set by Stripe on Stripe's own checkout pages to process payments. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

If we introduce analytics or marketing technologies in the future, we will update this policy and obtain your consent first where the law requires it.

10. Security

We protect personal data using measures including encryption in transit, private storage buckets, row-level security on our database, least-privilege service credentials, and isolation of payment data at Stripe. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a personal-data breach occurs, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority and affected users where required by law, including within 72 hours of becoming aware where the GDPR applies.

11. Children

The Service is for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect or process the personal data of children. If we learn that an account belongs to someone under 18, we delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact support@vankode.com and we will act promptly.

12. Changes and contact

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a revised "Last updated" date and, for material changes, provide additional notice where reasonably feasible. For any privacy question or request, contact support@vankode.com.
